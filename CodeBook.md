## Activity Labels

During the test subject was: 
* `WALKING` (value `1`): walking 
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`): walking up the stair 
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): walking down a staircase 
* `SITTING` (value `4`): sitting 
* `STANDING` (value `5`): standing 
* `LAYING` (value `6`): laying down
